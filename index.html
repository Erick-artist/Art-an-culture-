About Me

Erick Kyalo Stephen is a self taught artist based out of Nairobi Kenya.

Since the age of 5 Erick knew he was a painter. He grew up with the love and passion for art but decided to pursue other interests after high school.

At his early 20's Erick decided to perfect his art skills. He decided to teach himself to paint and pursue his childhood dream of becoming a great Artist.
